About Aristotle:

Aristotle was a Greek philosopher and polymath who lived in the 4th century BCE. He was a student of Plato and one of the most influential thinkers in Western philosophy

Education:

Plato's Academy: At the age of 17 or 18, Aristotle traveled to Athens to study at Plato's Academy, one of the most prestigious institutions of learning in the ancient.
Student of Plato: Aristotle spent nearly 20 years at the Academy, first as a student and later as a teacher and researcher.
